**Tip for practice:**
- When denominators are the same, subtract the numerators and keep the denominator
- For mixed numbers, convert to improper fractions first or borrow from the whole number
- Always reduce your answer to lowest terms
- If the fraction part being subtracted is larger, you need to borrow 1 from the whole number
