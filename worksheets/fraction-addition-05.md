**Tip for practice:**
- When fractions have different denominators, find the Least Common Denominator (LCD)
- List multiples of each denominator until you find a common one
- Convert both fractions using the LCD
- Add the numerators and keep the denominator
- Always reduce your answer to lowest terms
- Convert improper fractions to mixed numbers
- Example: 1/3 + 1/2 → LCD = 6 → 2/6 + 3/6 = 5/6
