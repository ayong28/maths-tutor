**Tips for practice:**
- Combine only like terms (same variables with same powers)
- Add the coefficients (numbers in front of the variables)
- Leave the variable part unchanged
- Group like terms together before adding to avoid mistakes
- Terms with different variables cannot be combined
- Watch for different powers: x and x² are NOT like terms
- Example: 2a + 5b + 7a = (2a + 7a) + 5b = 9a + 5b
