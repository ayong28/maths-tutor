**Tip for practice:**
- When fractions have different denominators, find the Least Common Denominator (LCD)
- Convert both fractions to the same denominator
- Add the numerators and keep the denominator
- Always reduce your answer to lowest terms
- Convert improper fractions to mixed numbers when the numerator is greater than the denominator
- Example: 1/2 + 1/3 → LCD = 6 → 3/6 + 2/6 = 5/6
