**Tips for practice:**
- Multiply the coefficients (numbers) together
- Multiply or add the powers of the variables
- When multiplying same variables: x × x = x², x × x² = x³ (add the powers)
- For powers of expressions like (4a)²: calculate 4² = 16 for the coefficient, and a² = a² for the variable
- Example: 3x × 5x² = (3 × 5) × (x × x²) = 15 × x³ = 15x³
- Remember the power rule: when multiplying same bases, add the exponents
