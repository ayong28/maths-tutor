**Tip for practice:**
- When denominators are the same, subtract the numerators and keep the denominator
- For mixed numbers with the same denominator, subtract the whole numbers and fractions separately
- If the fraction part being subtracted is larger than the first fraction, borrow 1 from the whole number
- Example: 2 1/4 - 3/4 → Borrow to get 1 5/4 - 3/4 = 1 2/4 = 1 1/2
- Always reduce your answer to lowest terms
